Bariatric Chia Pudding

This quick chia seed pudding is ideal for bariatric patients and those on a high-protein diet.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Stevia to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a jar.
  2. Stir well and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Enjoy chilled the next day.
